Quinoa Salad

It’s made simply with fresh cucumber, red bell pepper, red onion, chickpeas, fresh parsley and a garlicky olive oil and lemon dressing.

Posted September 23,2020 by Umapreethi

Ingredients

  • Avacado - 2
  • Cucumber - 2
  • Tomato
  • Red Onion
  • Cilantro
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon
  • Salt
  • Pepper
It’s made simply with fresh cucumber, red bell pepper, red onion, chickpeas, fresh parsley and a garlicky olive oil and lemon dressing. It’s made simply with fresh cucumber, red bell pepper, red onion, chickpeas, fresh parsley and a garlicky olive oil and lemon dressing.

Procedure

  1. Cut the avacado,cucumber,tomato into small pieces. thinly slice the onion.

  2. In a mixing bowl, add diced avacado,tomato,cucumber,onions and cilantro.

  3. For dressing, add two table spoons of olive oil, squeeze a lemon and make the juice and combine both.

  4. Finally add dressing to mixture, add some salt and pepper as required and mix everything.
